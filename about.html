Our Story

The Armenian Cultural Centre at the willoughby was established in 1985. What started as a small meeting space has grown into a vibrant hub where people of all ages and backgrounds come together.

Over the years, we've hosted thousands of events, supported countless local groups, and watched generations grow up within our walls. We believe that strong communities are built on connection, and we're proud to provide the space where those connections happen.

Today, we continue to evolve — adding new programmes, improving our facilities, and finding new ways to serve our diverse and wonderful community.

Our Values

Everything we do is guided by these core principles

Inclusivity

🌱

Community

We believe in the power of people coming together. Strong communities start with strong connections.

💜

Wellbeing

We support the physical, mental, and social wellbeing of everyone who walks through our doors.

Accessibility
